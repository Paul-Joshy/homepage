Vim tips

Behavior Action 
:set virtualedit=all
 Treat the editor as a canvas. Paste blocks anywhere without vim converting them into lines. 
gJ
 Join multiple lines 
:tabe
 Open new tab 
gt
 Toggle between tabs 
g?
 Ceasar cypher existing doc 
:%s/></>\r</g
 Prettify HTML 
:s/\S\+/\\="\\"" . submatch(0) . \\"\r"/g
Turn every word into a new line and wrap them in double quotes
