Basic phrases

Here's a cheat sheet of basic phrases you can use, to get around.

Phrase Pronunciation English Translation
Namaskaram! Nah-mah-ska-rahm Hello!
Pinne kaanam! Pin-nay kah-num See you later!
Suprabhaatham Soo-prah-bhaa-tham Good morning
Shubha raathri Shu-bhah raa-three Good night
Ente peru _____ aanu En-tay pay-roo _____ aa-noo My name is _____
Sughamano? Soo-gha-mah-no? How are you?
Enikku sukhamanu En-ik-koo soo-kha-mah-noo I’m fine
Enikku sukhamalla En-ik-koo soo-kha-mah-la I’m not fine
Valare nanni Vah-lah-ray Nun-nee Thank you very much.
Kshamikkanam Ksha-mik-ka-num Please forgive me.
Ithu evideya? I-thoo eh-vi-day-ah? Where is this?
Ethrayayi? Eth-ra-yaa-yee? How much?
Enikku manassilaayilla En-ik-koo ma-nas-si-laa-yil-la I didn't understand
Angane alla Anga-nay al-la It’s not like that
Athu thanne Ah-thoo thaan-nay Exactly!
