Question words in malayalam.

These are the wh questions in Malayalam.

Phrase English Meaning Example Sentence
Entha What? Ninte peru entha? (What is your name?)
Aaru Who? Aaru ivide vannu? (Who came here?)
Evide Where? Evideya ni poyi? (Where did you go?)
Eppozhe When? Eppozhe aanu sadya? (When is lunch?)
Endhinu Why? Nee endhinu vanna? (Why did you come?)
Engane How? Ninte sadhanam engane poyi? (How did your thing go?)
Ethra How much / How many? Ithinte vila ethra? (How much is this?)
Ethu Which? Ethu pusthakam aanu ninakku ishtam? (Which book do you like?)

How to ask questions in Malayalam?

Asking questions in Malayalam is easy. You just have to change the end of your phrase.

Phrase English meaning Suffix used
Sugham aano? Are you fine? -o
Sugham aanu I'm fine. -u
Sugham alle? Are you not fine? -lle
Sugham alla I'm not fine -lla
