My favourite razors, idioms, sieves, and the like.

Of two competing theories, the simpler explanation of an entity is to be preferred.
— Occam's razor

Never attribute to malice that which is adequately explained by stupidity.
— Hanlon's razor

What can be claimed without evidence can also be dismissed without evidence,
— Hitchen's razor

Extraordinary claims require extraordinary evidence
— Carl Sagan

When you have eliminated the impossible, whatever remains, however improbable, must be the truth
— Sherlock Holmes (Abductive Reasoning)
